# Privacy Policy for ScheduleMe Bot

**Last Updated:** January 16, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to ScheduleMe Bot ("Bot", "we", "us", or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Telegram bot.

By using ScheduleMe Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

When using our bot, we may collect the following information:

- **Telegram Identifier:** Your unique Telegram ID
- **Username:** Your name and username on Telegram
- **Contact Information:** Phone number (if you voluntarily provide it)
- **Profile Information:** User or specialist profile data
- **Booking Data:** Information about your appointments with specialists
- **Messages:** Text messages you send to the bot

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data:** Information about your interactions with the bot (commands, button clicks)
- **Technical Data:** Date and time of requests, interface language
- **Geolocation:** Location (only if you provide it to search for nearby specialists)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive basic information about your account from Telegram according to their [Privacy Policy](https://telegram.org/privacy).

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Providing Services:** Managing appointments, finding specialists, displaying schedules
- **Communications:** Sending confirmations, appointment reminders, and updates
- **Personalization:** Tailoring user experience to your needs
- **Analytics:** Improving bot functionality and fixing errors
- **Support:** Responding to inquiries and providing technical support
- **Security:** Preventing fraud and abuse

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, we process your personal data on the following legal grounds:

- **Consent:** You have given explicit consent to data processing
- **Contract Performance:** To provide booking services
- **Legitimate Interests:** To improve services and prevent fraud
- **Legal Obligations:** To comply with data protection laws

## 5. How We Store Your Information

### 5.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored in:

- **Supabase (PostgreSQL):** Secure cloud database with encryption
- **Telegram Servers:** For message exchange (according to Telegram's policy)

### 5.2 Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational security measures:

- Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Data encryption at rest
- Row Level Security (RLS) in database
- Limited access to personal data
- Regular backups
- Security monitoring

### 5.3 Retention Period

We retain your personal data:

- **Active Users:** While you use the bot
- **Inactive Accounts:** Up to 2 years from last activity
- **After Deletion:** Some data may be retained up to 30 days for backups
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law

## 6. Disclosure to Third Parties

### 6.1 When We Share Data

We may disclose your information:

- **To Specialists:** If you make a booking, the specialist receives your contact details
- **Service Providers:** Supabase (database hosting), Telegram (bot infrastructure)
- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly agree to disclosure
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or court order

### 6.2 International Transfers

Your data may be transferred and stored on servers outside Ukraine or the EU. We ensure an appropriate level of protection in accordance with GDPR and local laws.

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- ✅ **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your data
- ✅ **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- ✅ **Right to Erasure:** Delete your account and data ("right to be forgotten")
- ✅ **Right to Restriction:** Restrict processing of your data
- ✅ **Right to Portability:** Receive your data in a structured format
- ✅ **Right to Object:** Object to data processing
- ✅ **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time

### 7.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise your rights:

1. **Account Deletion:** Use the `/deleteaccount` command in the bot (feature in development)
2. **Data Request:** Contact [@book_me_io](https://t.me/book_me_io)
3. **Other Requests:** Contact us through the details below

We will respond to your request within 30 days.

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

Our Telegram bot **does not use cookies** as it operates within Telegram. However, if you navigate to the ScheduleMe website through the bot, other privacy policies may apply.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our bot is not intended for persons under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us for deletion.

## 10. Changes to Privacy Policy

- Notification in the bot
- Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of the document

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
